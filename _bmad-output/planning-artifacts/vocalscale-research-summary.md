# VocalScale Research Summary - Competitor Analysis

**Date:** 2026-02-19
**Purpose:** Identify missing features and improvement opportunities

---

## Current VocalScale Features

### Frontend Capabilities
- ✅ Authentication (Google OAuth, email/password)
- ✅ Landing page with pricing tiers
- ✅ Blog system with SEO optimization
- ✅ Dashboard modules:
  - Call logs with AI summary & audio playback
  - Chat/skills configuration
  - Appointment scheduling
  - Orders management
  - Reviews with AI summary
  - Billing with usage breakdown
  - Settings (voice, notifications, integrations)
- ✅ Business setup (details, hours, inventory, services)
- ✅ Voice setup (phone numbers, subaccounts)

### Pricing
- **Starter:** $69-99/month (300 minutes, standard voice, 1 number)
- **Professional:** $118-169/month (1000 minutes, premium HD voice, up to 5 numbers, sentiment analysis, CRM integrations)

## Competitor Landscape

### Key Competitors
| Platform | Differentiators |
|----------|----------------|
| **RingCentral AI** | Enterprise-scale omnichannel (voice + digital), intelligent routing |
| **Answering Agent** | Unlimited calls, customizable scripts, $99/mo |
| **Smith.ai** | Voice cloning, custom AI receptionist |
| **Play.ai** | Voice cloning (your own voice or custom) |
| **Rosie** | 7-day free trial, AI answering system |
| **Crescendo AI** | Omnichannel (chat, email, SMS, voice), sentiment intelligence |
| **Retell AI** | Social, messaging, email, web chat, voice unified |
| **Resemble AI** | Voice cloning (60 sec audio), emotional control, multilingual |

### Common Features in Competitors

1. **Voice Cloning/Custom Voices** - Smith.ai, Play.ai, Resemble
2. **Omnichannel Support** - Voice + SMS + Chat + Email (Crescendo, Retell, CloudTalk)
3. **Advanced Analytics** - Sentiment, call metrics, omnichannel analytics (Crescendo, Retell)
4. **CRM Integrations** - HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive (mentioned in VocalScale Pro but not detailed)
5. **Custom Scripts/Prompts** - Granular control over AI behavior (Answering Agent)
6. **Free Trials** - 7-14 days (Rosie, many others)
7. **Real-time Transcription** - Live call whisper/transcripts (RingCentral, others)
8. **Team Collaboration** - Multiple users, roles, permissions (enterprise platforms)

## Strategic Recommendations

### Quick Wins (1-2 sprints)
1. **Free Trial Flow** - Add prominent 14-day trial to pricing page
2. **Real-time Transcription** - Upgrade from AI summary post-call to live whisper
3. **SMS Channel** - Add SMS messaging alongside voice

### Strategic Wins (2-3 sprints)
4. **Voice Cloning** - Allow businesses to upload voice samples
5. **Omnichannel Dashboard** - Unified view of voice, SMS, chat
6. **Advanced Analytics** - Add sentiment trends, call metrics, KPIs

### Differentiators (3-4 sprints)
7. **Custom Script Builder** - Drag-and-drop AI behavior control
8. **CRM Integration Marketplace** - HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zapier
9. **Team Management** - Multi-user with roles and permissions
10. **Live Chat Widget** - Embeddable chat for websites

## Competitive Positioning

**VocalScale Strengths:**
- Clean, modern UI
- Existing analytics (AI summaries)
- Appointment & order management (unique feature)
- Inventory management (unique feature)
- Competitive pricing

**VocalScale Weaknesses:**
- No voice cloning (common in competitors)
- No omnichannel (standard in top platforms)
- No free trial (conversion barrier)
- Limited analytics visibility
- Unknown CRM integrations (mentioned but not detailed)
